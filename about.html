Overview

The G20 Summit is an annual international forum for governments and central bank governors from 19 countries and the European Union. It aims to discuss and coordinate global economic policy. The 2023 summit, hosted by India, will be a crucial gathering of leaders to address pressing issues facing the world.

Objectives

The primary objectives of the G20 Summit 2023 include fostering international economic cooperation, promoting sustainable development, and addressing challenges such as climate change, global health, and economic inequality. Leaders will engage in discussions and negotiations to find common ground on these critical issues.
