1. Economic Stability

The G20 Summit 2023 will address challenges related to global economic stability. Leaders will discuss measures to promote sustainable economic growth, financial resilience, and cooperation on international trade.

2. Sustainable Development

Sustainable development is a key focus area, encompassing environmental sustainability, social development, and economic inclusivity. The summit aims to explore strategies for achieving sustainable and equitable growth.

3. Climate Change

G20 leaders will engage in discussions on combating climate change and promoting environmental conservation. The summit aims to foster collaboration on initiatives that address the urgent challenges of climate change.
